This is the data heart of T-PIMS™. It stores everything—network structure, ROW (Right of Way) details, pipeline sections, construction data, and O&M records. Pipelines are segmented by region, thickness, state, and more to give you a crystal-clear picture of every asset in your network.

CP is vital in managing corrosion risk. This module handles both frequent surveys (like PSP monitoring, DC/AC current density) and long-interval diagnostics (CIPS, DCVG, Soil Inspection). Visualize your results through charts, heatmaps, and benchmarks to easily spot anomalies and plan proactive maintenance.

See your entire asset on a map, synced with all operational data. Whether using your existing GIS database or a custom-built solution from AE, this module lets you zoom from global to granular—integrating insights from every module seamlessly into your geospatial view.

Like a project manager built into the platform, this module keeps track of surveys, inspections, and maintenance tasks. Each activity is assigned a traceable ID. Users can view, schedule, and receive alerts—customized for deadlines, stakeholders, and organizational needs.

View, analyse and interact with high-resolution ILI data. The module shows pipeline features—welds, fittings, metal loss, and more—synchronized with GIS maps. Visual analytics reveal corrosion clusters, dent positions, and threat zones in real-time with detailed graphs and location mapping.

The centrepiece of T-PIMS™, this module processes all collected data to deliver actionable risk intelligence. Features include:

  • User-defined threats and consequences.
  • Spill scenario simulation and HCA identification.
  • Semi-quantitative and quantitative risk scoring.
  • Threat-specific likelihood and consequence modelling.
  • Cost of Failure (CoF) assessments across people, environment, legal, economic, and operational domains.
  • Targeted mitigation recommendations by threat type.
